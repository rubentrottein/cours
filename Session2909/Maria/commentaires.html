Beautiful and quiet apartment.
The pictures of the apartment are true to the reality. The layout of the apartment is perfect for 3 guests. The bedroom is separated from the living-room by sliding panels made out of a very nice and light material. The kitchen is small but very practical. The building is quiet, and well located. It’s within walking distance to bakeries, fruit and vegatable stands, and supermarkets, among other shops. It’s very close to the Metro Jules Joffrin, and it is easy to walk to the Sacré-Coeur, and all the charming little streets of Montmartre. The bonus is of course the balcony overlooking a garden where pigeons and other birds almost make you forget you are in Paris. Victor, the owner, is very approachable. He answers messages very quickly with all the details needed. He is very reliable, and easy to talk to. My husband, my son and I enjoyed very much our stay in Victor’s apartment.

Earl P.

Thoroughly enjoyable! We rented this apartment in the Montmartre section of Paris for nine nights and completely enjoyed our stay! We loved sitting outside in the mornings and evenings on its nice quiet balcony overlooking yards with plants and trees rather than a city street. The apartment also has a nicely equipped kitchenette, a separate bathroom with a shower, sink, and washing machine, a WC (which does not have a sink), a good TV and excellent Wi-Fi. Everything in the apartment was clean and in excellent shape. The immediate neighborhood is safe and quiet, within easy walking distance of good boulangeries, restaurants, and two Metro lines (4 & 12), as well as the Basilique du Sacré-Coeur de Montmartre and the Dali Museum (Espace Dalí). Victor is friendly and helpful. He met us soon after our taxi dropped us from Charles de Gaulle airport. We would highly recommend this apartment to anyone vacationing in Paris, and would definitely stay here again if we are fortunate enough to come back.
Juillet 2018
